November 2023

I participated in the Consumer AI Hackathon in London, hosted by Eleven Labs and backed by industry heavyweights like a16z, Synthesia, and AI Tinkerers. This event brought together forward-thinking minds to create innovative consumer AI solutions, with judges including Eleven Labs' CEO Mati Staniszewski and a16z General Partner Sriram Krishnan. It was a fast-paced weekend packed with ideation, development, and pitching.

Our team's idea, 6Sense, stood out and won the AI Tinkerers community prize. The project was essentially "a set of eyes inside your ears" - an assistive AI solution for real-time contextual awareness. 6Sense uses live video feeds, action recognition, and real-time feedback to offer enhanced auditory insights. Designed to help users navigate the world with more confidence, it's especially useful for those with vision impairments, ADHD, or dementia, as well as general users needing hands-free assistance or safety alerts.
