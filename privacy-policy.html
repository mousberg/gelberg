Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit gelberg.co. We act as a data controller for any personal information collected through this website.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Your explicit consent for analytics cookies
  • Our legitimate interests in maintaining and improving our website

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Marketing Cookies (_ga, _ga_#): We use Google Analytics cookies to understand website traffic and usage patterns. These cookies are set only with your explicit consent.

Data Transfer Outside the EU

Please note that Google Analytics data is processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with Google.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Analyze website traffic patterns (only with your consent)
  • Improve user experience based on aggregated analytics data
  • Monitor website performance

Cookie Policy

We use only two types of Google Analytics cookies:

  • _ga: Used to distinguish unique users
  • _ga_#: Used to maintain session status
  • Both cookies are classified as non-essential analytics cookies
  • Are only set after obtaining your explicit consent
  • Collect anonymous data about how you use our website
  • Are stored for up to 2 years
  • Transfer data to Google servers in the United States
  • Can be rejected without any loss of website functionality

What We Don't Do

For transparency, we want to clarify that we:

  • Don't use any essential/necessary cookies
  • Don't use any advertising or marketing cookies
  • Don't share your data with third parties except Google Analytics
  • Don't use cookies for any purpose other than anonymous analytics

Your Rights

Under GDPR and ePrivacy regulations, you have the right to:

  • Receive transparent information about how your data is processed
  • Access your personal data in a machine-readable format
  • Refuse non-essential cookies without any impact on website functionality
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request rectification or erasure of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
  • Not be subject to automated decision-making

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months, after which it is automatically deleted. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

International Data Transfers

When we transfer your data outside the EU/EEA (such as to Google Analytics in the United States), we ensure appropriate safeguards through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Data breach notification procedures

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights under GDPR, please contact:

  • Data Controller: David Gelberg
  • Email: davidgelberg@gmail.com
  • Response Time: Within 30 days of receiving your request
  • Supervisory Authority: Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) - ico.org.uk

Changes to This Policy

We regularly review and update this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Significant changes will be notified to you directly.
